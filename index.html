About me

I graduated with a M.S. in Computer Science from Lehigh University, worked in the DASH Lab with Prof. Yu Yang.

My research there focused on human-computer interaction, and human-AI interaction. We developed the GIDEA framework, a 3D simulation platform built with Unity for Mac, and Vision Pro, designed to replicate and evaluate human-assistant interactions in a controlled yet realistic virtual environment.

I am also working with Prof. Zilong Pan starting from Nov 2024 on StemPal Web Pack, aiming to leverage AI on helping students learning and instructors teaching.

I am a curious and fast learner passionate about building human-centered and adaptive AI systems that enhance everyday experiences. My research interests include human-AI interaction, behavior- and context-powered personalization, and intelligent assistant frameworks that understand users' goals, preferences, and routines to offer timely, natural, and context-aware support—whether at home, at work, or on the move. I aim to design AI technologies that integrate seamlessly into daily life, improving how people interact with devices, environments, and each other.
